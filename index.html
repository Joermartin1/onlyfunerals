How It Works

Simple. Transparent.
On your terms.

Three steps. No surprises. No one contacts you without your permission.

Tell us what you need

A short guided intake: service type, location, timing, and what matters most. One question at a time. Takes under three minutes.

02

Cost Transparency

What a funeral actually costs in America

Most families make a $10,000 decision in 24 hours with no prior research. Here's what fair looks like.

Service Type
National Average
What's Typically Included
Direct Cremation
$800 – $2,500
Transportation, cremation, basic urn. No ceremony required.
Graveside Service
$4,000 – $7,000
Brief ceremony at cemetery. Casket, burial, minimal staff.
Traditional Burial
$9,000 – $15,000
Viewing, funeral, burial, casket, plot. Most common upsell scenario.

Source: NFDA 2024 survey data. Costs vary significantly by geography and provider.
